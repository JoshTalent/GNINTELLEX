About Us

GN IntelleX is a leading software company based in Kigali, Rwanda, specializing in graphic design, web development, mobile app development, photography, and video graphics. Co-founded by industry experts Jean Marie GATARE and Josue NTWARI, we offer innovative solutions that help businesses thrive in the digital world. Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to delivering high-quality services that exceed our clients' expectations. Whether you need a stunning website, captivating visuals, or cutting-edge mobile apps, GN IntelleX is your go-to partner for all your software needs. Contact us today to take your business to the next level.
